## 1. Executive Summary

### What is PropprTeamBot?

**@PropprTeamBot** is a specialized Telegram bot that identifies value betting opportunities in **team prop betting markets**. While the Player Bot focuses on individual player performance, the Team Bot analyzes collective team statistics to find edges in match and team totals.

### Key Value Proposition

> **"The team bot powers the player bot"** - Jay (Prof. X), Creator

The Team Bot is the **foundation** of the Proppr ecosystem. Without team projections, there are no player projections. The same statistical models that predict team tackles, shots, and corners are used to derive individual player expectations.

## 5. FAQs (25+ Questions)

### General Questions

**Q1: Is the Team Bot different from the Player Bot?**
A: Yes. Team Bot analyzes collective team statistics; Player Bot focuses on individual player props. Team Bot powers the Player Bot's projections.

**Q2: How accurate are team projections?**
A: About 65-75% for major markets. The model factors in opponent strength, historical performance, playing style, and more.

**Q3: What's the relationship between Team Bot and Player Bot?**
A: "With no team bot there's no player." Team projections form the foundation for individual player expectations.

**Q4: What sports does the Team Bot cover?**
A: Football/soccer only, with 100+ leagues worldwide.

**Q5: Can I use both bots together?**
A: Absolutely. Use Team Bot for match-level analysis, Player Bot for individual selections.

### Technical Questions

**Q6: Why am I not getting alerts?**
A: Check:
1. Have you sent `/start` to @PropprTeamBot?
2. Are your settings too restrictive?
3. Is the market enabled in `/markets`?
4. Is your subscription tier active?

**Q7: What are predicted lines?**
A: Comprehensive team stat projections including goals, corners, cards, shots, fouls, tackles, offsides, throw-ins, free kicks, and more.

**Q8: How often are projections updated?**
A: Continuously as new data becomes available. Major updates typically hourly.

**Q9: What's the difference between Last 5 and Last 10?**
A: Changes the sample size for historical calculations. Last 10 more stable; Last 5 more recent-form focused.

**Q10: Does the bot work in-play?**
A: The `(feature removed)` command provides in-play data, but pre-match alerts are the core feature.

---

### Betting Questions

**Q11: How is value calculated?**
A: `Value % = ((Bookmaker Odds / Model Odds) - 1) × 100`

**Q12: What does the recommended stake mean?**
A: Stakes factor in value + probability. Higher value + higher probability = higher stake recommendation.

**Q13: Can I bet on unders?**
A: Yes, under alerts are supported. Enable in settings.

**Q14: What's a Freeze Alert?**
A: Sky Acca Freeze opportunities where you can lock in odds before lineups confirm. Based on Score First and Lead Anytime probabilities.

**Q15: What are XI Alerts?**
A: Notifications when starting lineups are confirmed (typically 75 minutes before kickoff).

**Q16: What's Team Most?**
A: Predictions for which team will have the most corners, cards, shots, etc. in a match.

### Feature-Specific Questions

**Q21: What leagues are covered for Freeze Alerts?**
A: Major leagues including Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1, Eredivisie, Championship, and more (20+ leagues).

**Q22: How do Live Commands work?**

**Q23: What's the difference between Team Totals and Match Totals?**
A: Team Totals = one team's contribution (e.g., Liverpool corners). Match Totals = combined both teams.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ DO's

1. **DO use the tutorial**
   > "I strongly recommend doing the tutorial as it guides you through how to set up the bot and the way it works"

2. **DO consider the probability, not just value**
   > "Great value but the model odds being 7.18 does still mean the model only gives him a 14% chance"

3. **DO check multiple bookmakers**
   - Bot links to Bet365, but better prices often exist elsewhere

4. **DO use /fixture for deep dives**
   > "/fixture is the most popular command it sends you alerts for all the value props for a single game in less than a minute"

5. **DO factor in team rotation**
   > "If you used the /today command you would of seen this... all the rotation prior to the game"

6. **DO track your bets**
   - Use `/track` or external spreadsheet for accountability

7. **DO stake consistently**
   > "Don't be scared to go high volume just make sure your stake effectively and consistently"


9. **DO use Team Most for comparative analysis**
   - Great for "which team will have more" markets

10. **DO enable XI alerts for freeze betting**
    - Lineup confirmation timing is crucial

11. **DO cross-reference with weather**
    - Conditions significantly affect team totals

12. **DO use Live commands for over monitoring**
    - Catch early wins before full-time

13. **DO specialize in specific leagues**
    - Better than spreading across unfamiliar competitions

14. **DO review first half markets separately**
    - Different dynamics than full match

15. **DO take screenshots of alerts**
    - Useful for tracking and dispute resolution

---

### ❌ DON'Ts

1. **DON'T take every alert**
   > "Taking every alert it sends isn't the way to go"

2. **DON'T expect every bet to win**
   > "Every alert isn't a guaranteed winner"

3. **DON'T chase losses**
   - The model is about long-term value, not short-term wins

4. **DON'T ignore recommended stakes**
   - They're calculated based on value + probability

5. **DON'T bet on negative value**
   > "Taking negative value plays is what the bookmakers want"

6. **DON'T spam /fixture**
   - It can lock the bot for other users (though less so than in beta)

7. **DON'T rely solely on hit rates**
   > "If it was just based on hit rates the model odds would be like 1.25"

8. **DON'T ignore league context**
   - Serie A cards ≠ Premier League cards

9. **DON'T bet blindly on freeze alerts**
   - Research the fixture context first

10. **DON'T overlook team totals for value**
    - Often better value than match totals

11. **DON'T forget weather impacts**
    - Heavy rain changes everything

12. **DON'T chase live odds**
    - Bot is pre-match focused

13. **DON'T ignore the rating in freeze alerts**
    - C+ opportunities are marginal at best

14. **DON'T bet above your unit size**
    - Even on A+ rated opportunities

15. **DON'T mute entire leagues permanently**
    - May miss valuable opportunities

## 12. Explainers

### How Team Projections Work

The "Cebro" model analyzes:

1. **Historical Performance** (Last 5 or 10 games)
2. **Opponent Strength** (defensive/offensive ratings)
3. **Playing Style** (possession, pressing intensity)
4. **Match Context** (home/away, derby, stakes)
5. **Lineup Quality** (confirmed or predicted)

**Calculation Process:**
```
Raw Hit Rate → Opponent Adjustment → Style Modifier 
    → Model Odds → Value Calculation → Stake Recommendation
```

---

### How Freeze Alerts Work

**Sky Acca Freeze** allows you to lock in prices before lineups are confirmed.

**The Model Calculates:**
1. **Score First Chance:** Probability of scoring the first goal
2. **Lead Anytime Chance:** Probability of taking the lead at any point
3. **Combined Rating:** A+ (best) to C- (marginal)

**When to Use:**
- You believe the odds will shorten after lineups
- Early value opportunities
- Before significant news breaks

---

### How XI Alerts Work

**Process:**
1. System polls SportMonks API every 7 seconds
2. Detects when lineups are confirmed (75 mins before kickoff)
3. Sends notification with:
   - Confirmed starting XI
   - Formation
   - Any significant changes

**Why It Matters:**
- Lineup changes dramatically affect projections
- Key player absences create new value
- Unexpected starters may be mispriced

---

### How Live Commands Work

**Features:**
1. **Live Fixtures:** Currently playing matches
2. **Real-Time Stats:** Current score, shots, corners, cards
3. **Early Win Detection:** Over bets graded when threshold exceeded
4. **Batch Processing:** Up to 50 fixtures per API call

**Supported Markets for Early Grading:**
- Goals (Team/Match totals)
- Shots on Target
- Total Shots
- Corners
- Cards/Bookings
